Kawasaki History

Here are some other milestones in Kawasaki's history:

1926

The company was established on January 28, 1926.

1960

: Kawasaki acquired Meguro motorcycles, a major player in the Japanese motorcycle manufacturing industry

1961

: Kawasaki produced its first motorcycle, the B8 125cc two-stroke

1906

: Kawasaki opened a new factory and began diversifying its products

2019

: Kawasaki's factory team, the Kawasaki Racing Team, won the Suzuka 8 Hours Endurance Road Race for the first time since 1993
